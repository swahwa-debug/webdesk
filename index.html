Screen Share

Share your screen between two PCs

How to share your screen:

  1. Click "Generate Connection Code" to create a connection offer
  2. Copy the generated code and send it to the person who wants to view your screen
  3. Wait for them to send you their connection code
  4. Paste their code in the "Viewer's Code" field and click "Connect"
  5. Once connected, click "Start Sharing" to share your screen
